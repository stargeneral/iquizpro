Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period with no further charges.
What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe — a secure, industry-standard payment processor. We never store your card details.
Can I upgrade from Premium to Pro?
Yes. Contact us and we'll handle the plan change. Automated plan switching via the customer portal is coming soon.
What happens to unused AI generations?
Unused generations reset at the start of each billing month — they do not roll over. This keeps costs predictable for everyone.
Is there a free trial?
Our Basic plan is free forever with 5 AI quiz generations per month — no credit card required. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans.
Do you offer discounts for schools or non-profits?
Yes — contact us at hello@iquizpro.com to discuss educational and non-profit pricing.
